#### 3. Curriculum/Course Structure (B.Tech)
IIT Roorkee implemented a New Education Policy (NEP 2020) compliant curriculum in the 2023-24 session. All branches share a common first-year core.

*   **Common First-Year Core Subjects:** Mathematics (I & II), Engineering Physics, Engineering Chemistry, Programming for Problem Solving, Soft Skills, Tinkering and Mentoring, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML), Data Science, and Indian Knowledge System (IKS).

*   **Departmental Core Subjects by Branch:**
    *   **CSE:** Data Structures & Algorithms, Theory of Computation, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Database Management Systems (DBMS), Compiler Design, Computer Organization & Architecture.
    *   **ECE:** Semiconductor Devices, Digital Logic Design, Signals and Systems, Electromagnetic Theory, Control Systems, Principles of Communication, VLSI Design, Analog Circuits.
    *   **EE:** Network Analysis, Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Fundamentals of Electronics, Microprocessors, Control Engineering.
    *   **ME:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Material Science, Machine Design, Heat & Mass Transfer, Theory of Production Processes, Kinematics & Dynamics of Machines.
    *   **Civil Engineering:** Structural Analysis, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Surveying, Fluid Mechanics, Environmental Engineering.
    *   **Chemical Engineering:** Chemical Process Calculations, Thermodynamics, Heat Transfer, Mass Transfer, Chemical Reaction Engineering, Process Dynamics & Control.
    *   **Metallurgical & Materials Engineering:** Physical Metallurgy, Metallurgical Thermodynamics, Mechanical Metallurgy, Extraction of Metals, Materials Characterization.

#### 4. Campus Life & Infrastructure
*   **Sports Facilities:**
    *   **Lal Bahadur Shastri (LBS) Stadium:** Main athletic track, cricket ground, and football field.
    *   **Major Dhyan Chand Stadium:** Primary facility for Hockey and Football.
    *   **Aquatics:** Olympic-size swimming pool.
    *   **Tennis:** Multiple synthetic and clay courts with floodlights.
    *   **Indoor Sports:** Dedicated complex for Badminton, Table Tennis, Squash, and Chess.
    *   **Fitness:** Fully equipped central Gymnasium and separate women’s gym.
    *   **Other:** Basketball and Volleyball courts (floodlit), Weightlifting arena, and Yoga Bhawan.

*   **Student Clubs:**
    *   **Technical Councils:** IMG (Information Management Group), SDSLabs (Software Development), Hobbies Club (Robotics, Aeromodelling, Star-gazing, Electronics sections), ACM/IEEE Chapters, SAE/ASME (Motorsports & Formula Student).
    *   **Cultural Councils:** Dramatics Section, Music Section (Institute Band), Choreography Section, Literary Section (Debating & Magazine), Fine Arts & Photography Section, Sanskrit Club, SPIC MACAY.
    *   **Entrepreneurship:** E-Cell (Entrepreneurship Cell).

#### 5. Startup Incubation
*   **Incubator Name:** TIDES (Technology Incubation and Entrepreneurship Development Society).
*   **Presence:** Active Technology Business Incubator (TBI) supported by MeitY and DST.
*   **Funding Facilities:**
    *   **Seed Fund:** Up to ₹25 Lakh for selected startups.
    *   **SIDBI Seed Fund:** Up to ₹1.5 Crore for iDEX-supported startups.
    *   **NIDHI PRAYAS:** Up to ₹10 Lakh for prototype development.
    *   **MeitY GENESIS:** Up to ₹10 Lakh for ICT ideation-stage startups.
*   **Notable Supported/Alumni Startups:**
    *   **Razorpay:** Founded by Harshil Mathur & Shashank Kumar (Fintech Unicorn).

#### 6. Wikipedia Cross-Verification & Additional Facts
*   **Historical Context:** Established in 1847 as the College of Civil Engineering (India's oldest engineering college). Renamed University of Roorkee in 1949 and converted to the 7th IIT in 2001.
*   **Campus Area:** Main campus spans 365 acres in Roorkee; additional campuses in Saharanpur (25 acres) and Greater Noida.
