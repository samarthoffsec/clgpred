### 3. Curriculum & Course Structure
The curriculum follows a credit-based system (approx. 155-170 credits total) consisting of Institute Core (IC), Departmental Core (DC), and Electives.

*   **Common Institute Core (First Year):** Calculus, Linear Algebra and Differential Equations, Physics for Engineers, Chemistry for Engineers, Introduction to Computing (C++), Engineering Drawing, Workshop Practice, Biology for Engineers.
*   **CSE Core:** Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, Computer Architecture, DBMS, Software Engineering, Discrete Mathematics.
*   **EE Core:** Signals and Systems, Digital Electronics, Control Systems, Power Systems, Microprocessors, Communication Engineering.
*   **ME Core:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Solid Mechanics, Kinematics of Machines, Manufacturing Processes, Heat Transfer.
*   **Chemical Core:** Fluid Mechanics, Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Heat Transfer, Mass Transfer, Chemical Reaction Engineering, Process Control.
*   **MME Core:** Metallurgical Thermodynamics, Transport Phenomena, Physical Metallurgy, Extractive Metallurgy, Iron and Steel Making, Materials Characterization.
*   **Civil Core:** Structural Analysis, Geotechnical Engineering, Surveying, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering.

*   **Sports Facilities:**

    *   **Track & Field:** 400m synthetic 8-lane athletic track.
    *   **Outdoor Grounds:** Floodlit Cricket ground with practice nets, Football field, Hockey field, and multiple Tennis, Basketball, and Volleyball courts.
    *   **Indoor:** Modern gymnasium, wooden-floored Badminton courts, Table Tennis, and facilities for Chess and Weightlifting.

### 6. Wikipedia Cross-Verification & Additional Details
*   **Foundation:** Established in 2008 as one of the eight second-generation IITs.
*   **Campus:** 525-acre permanent campus located on the banks of the Satluj River in Rupnagar, Punjab.
*   **Architecture:** Main entrance features four 41-foot-high stone-carved pillars inspired by the Indus Valley Civilization.
*   **Festivals:** **Zeitgeist** (Annual Cultural Fest) and **Advitiya** (Annual Technical Fest).
*   **Director:** Prof. Rajeev Ahuja.
*   **Motto:** *Dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt* (Deploy our Intellect on the Right Path).
