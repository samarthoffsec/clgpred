**3. Curriculum & Course Structure**
*   **Flagship Components:** 
    *   **Reverse Engineering:** Dismantling and reconstructing appliances to understand mechanisms.
    *   **Foundations of Design Practicum:** Hands-on product design in the first year.
    *   **Interactive Socio-Technical Practicum (ISTP):** Addressing societal issues in the Himalayan region with technical solutions.
*   **Core Subjects by Branch:**
    *   **CSE:** Operating Systems (OS), Information and Database Systems (DBMS), Data Structures and Algorithms, Computer Organization, Software Engineering, Theory of Computation.
    *   **Electrical (EE):** Signals and Systems, Control Systems, Power Electronics, Microprocessors and Microcontrollers, Communication Systems, Electromagnetic Theory.
    *   **Mechanical (ME):** Mechanics of Solids, Fluid Mechanics, Heat Transfer, Manufacturing Engineering, Kinematics and Dynamics of Machines.

**4. Campus Life & Infrastructure**
*   **Student Body:** Student Gymkhana (overseeing Technical, Cultural, Sports, Literary, and Research societies).
*   **Technical Clubs:** Robotronics (Robotics), Kamand Prompt (Programming), STAC (Space Tech), E-Cell (Entrepreneurship).
*   **Cultural Clubs:** Uhl Dance Crew, Shutterbugs (Photography), Music Club, Drama Club, Designauts.
*   **Adventure Clubs:** Hiking and Trekking (HnT) Club, Mountain Biking (MTB) Club.
*   **Sports Facilities:**
    *   **Outdoor:** Dedicated Cricket and Football grounds (South Campus); Hockey field, Basketball, Volleyball, and Lawn Tennis courts (North Campus).
    *   **Indoor:** Modern sports complex with Badminton, Squash, and Table Tennis courts.
    *   **Aquatics:** State-of-the-art swimming pool (North Campus); *Note: Not officially classified as Olympic-size (standard 25m).*
    *   **Athletics:** Functional athletic track and field pits; *Note: Professional 400m synthetic track is part of the master plan but not yet fully operational as of current official site listings.*

**5. Startup Incubation**
*   **Incubation Center:** **IIT Mandi Catalyst** (First Technology Business Incubator in Himachal Pradesh).
*   **Entrepreneurship Body:** **E-Cell IIT Mandi**.
*   **Key Facilities:** NIDHI-PRAYAS Lab, EWOK (Enabling Women of Kamand) initiative, iHub and HCI Foundation (Human-Computer Interaction).
*   **Notable Incubated Startups:** 
    *   **Enord:** AI-powered drones for autonomous navigation.
    *   **Wellnesys:** Creators of "YogiFi" (AI-integrated smart yoga mat).
    *   **Ésah Tea:** Specialized Himalayan organic tea brand.
    *   **Embright Infotech:** AI/XR platforms for Autism assistance.
    *   **Biofi Med:** Affordable medical diagnostic devices.

**6. Wikipedia Cross-Verification & Additional Facts**
*   **Established:** 2009 (Second-generation IIT).
*   **Motto:** "Scaling the heights!"
*   **Campus Area:** 538 acres in Kamand Valley, Mandi district.
*   **Geography:** Situated on the banks of the **Uhl River**; divided into North and South campuses.
*   **Academic Structure:** Operates through **Schools** (e.g., School of Computing and Electrical Engineering) rather than traditional departments.
*   **Mentoring Institute:** Originally mentored by IIT Roorkee.
