**3. Curriculum & Course Structure (B.Tech)**
- **Core Branches & Subjects:**
  - **Computer Science & Engineering:** Operating Systems (CS3101), Database Management Systems (CS2202), Computer Programming (C/C++), Data Structures, Algorithms.
  - **Electronics Engineering:** VLSI Design (EC 431), MOS Transistor Theory, CMOS Inverter, Digital Logic Design.
  - **Electrical Engineering:** Network Analysis and Synthesis, Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Control Systems, Power Electronics.
  - **Mechanical Engineering:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Heat and Mass Transfer, Mechanics of Deformable Solids (SOM), Machine Design.
  - **Civil Engineering:** Structural Analysis, Design of Concrete Structures, Geotechnical Engineering, Surveying, Transportation Engineering.
  - **Metallurgical Engineering:** Physical Metallurgy, Extractive Metallurgy, Metallurgical Thermodynamics, Structure of Materials.
  - **Mining Engineering:** Mining Geology, Mine Surveying, Rock Mechanics, Surface Mining, Mine Environment.
  - **Ceramic Engineering:** Glass Science and Technology, Refractories, Whitewares and Glazes, Electronic/Electro-Ceramics.
  - **Pharmaceutical Engineering:** Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacology, Pharmacognosy, Drug Design.
- **NEP 2020 Compliance:** Effective 2025-26, the curriculum features flexible pathways (Honours, Minors, Second Majors) and multiple exit options (Certificate after 1 yr, Diploma after 2 yrs).

**4. Campus Life & Infrastructure**
- **Student Body:** IIT (BHU) Gymkhana (governed by Dean of Student Affairs).
- **Sports Facilities:**
  - **Olympic-size Pool:** Taran Taal (50 meters length, inaugurated 2010).
  - **Student Activity Center (SAC):** Three-story facility with Indoor Badminton halls, Squash courts, Boxing and Taekwondo rings, Table Tennis arena.
  - **Kings' Pavilion:** Historic primary ground for Athletics, Cricket, Football, and Hockey.
  - **Others:** Synthetic/cement courts for Basketball, Volleyball, and Tennis.
- **Active Clubs:**
  - **Sports:** Cricket, Football, Hockey, Athletics, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, Tennis, Squash, Table Tennis, Boxing, Taekwondo, Weightlifting, Chess, Swimming.
  - **Technical/Cultural:** Cultural Council (Music, Dance, Lit), Science and Technology Council (Robotics, Aero-modelling, Astronomy), Film and Media Council (FMC).

**5. Startup Incubation**
- **Official Cell:** Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell), IIT (BHU) Varanasi.
- **Incubation Umbrella:** Ideation Innovation & Incubation (I-3) Foundation (I3F).
- **Key Incubators:** 
  - **MCIIE:** Malviya Centre for Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship.
  - **IDAPT-Hub Foundation:** Focuses on Data Analytics and Predictive Technologies.
  - **R-ABI:** Agri-Business Incubator.
  - **Cisco thingQbator:** Internal makerspace for IoT and hardware.
- **Notable Supported/Alumni Startups:** Zscaler (Jay Chaudhry), Physics Wallah (Alakh Pandey), Cricbuzz (Pankaj Chaparwal), Zostel (Dharamveer Singh Chouhan), ShopClues, Husk Power Systems, TRIPHAN Healthcare, DELBRONE Innovation.
- **Key Facilities:** IIT BHU Seed Fund (up to ₹25 Lakhs), NIDHI-PRAYAS grants, central instrument facility.

**6. Wikipedia Cross-Verification & Additional Details**
- **Established:** 1919 (as Banaras Engineering College - BENCO).
- **Motto:** *Saṃskāra hī śikṣā* (Education is Character).
- **Founder:** Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya.
- **IIT Status:** Converted from IT-BHU to IIT (BHU) Varanasi in 2012 via the Institutes of Technology (Amendment) Act.
- **Campus:** 1,300-acre residential campus shared with Banaras Hindu University.
- **NIRF 2025:** Officially ranked 10th in Engineering category (Source: NIRF India 2025).
