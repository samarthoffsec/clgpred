### 3. Curriculum / Course Structure
The curriculum follows a common first-year foundation for all B.Tech branches, followed by department-specific core subjects.
*   **Common First-Year Core:** Single Variable & Multi-Variable Calculus, Linear Algebra, Engineering Physics, Engineering Chemistry, Basic Electronics, Introduction to Programming (C/C++).
*   **CSE Core Subjects:** Discrete Mathematics, Data Structures, Digital Design, Computer Organization & Architecture, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Operating Systems (OS), Database Management Systems (DBMS), Compiler Design, Machine Learning.
*   **ECE/EEE Core Subjects:** Electric Circuits, Analog and Digital Electronics, Signals & Systems, Principles of Communication, VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, Control Systems.
*   **ME Core Subjects:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Solid Mechanics, Kinematics of Machinery, Manufacturing Technology, Heat and Mass Transfer.

### 4. Campus Life & Infrastructure
IIT Guwahati is managed by the Students' Gymkhana Council, which oversees sports, technical, and cultural activities.
*   **Sports Facilities:**
    *   **Olympic-size Swimming Pool:** 8-lane, 50-meter long facility managed by the Aquatics Club.
    *   **Athletic Track:** 400-meter premier synthetic track meeting international standards.
    *   **Indoor Stadium:** Features synthetic badminton courts, table tennis, and squash courts.
    *   **Other Courts/Grounds:** Flood-lit grounds for Cricket, Football, and Hockey; multiple synthetic Tennis, Basketball, and Volleyball courts.
*   **Active Clubs:**
    *   **Technical:** Robotics, Coding, Electronics, Aeromodelling, Consulting & Analytics.
    *   **Cultural/Arts:** Octaves (Music), Montage (Photography), Fine Arts, Literary, Anchoring, and Movie Club.
    *   **Sports:** Aquatics, Athletics, Badminton, Basketball, Chess, Football, Hockey, Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, Squash, Table Tennis, and Volleyball.

### 5. Startup Incubation
IIT Guwahati fosters a robust entrepreneurial ecosystem through its dedicated centers.
*   **Incubation Center:** Technology Incubation Centre (TIC).
*   **Student Body:** Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell), which organizes the annual "UDGAM" summit.
*   **Notable Supported Startups:**
    *   **DSRL (Da'Spatio Rhobotique Laboratory):** Deep-tech in Robotics and AI for Defense.
    *   **Primary Healthtech:** Affordable diagnostic medical devices.
    *   **Quant Solar:** Specialized in floating solar technology (Award-winning).
    *   **Logicknot:** Electronic Design Automation (EDA) software tools.
    *   **Medsolu:** Data-driven healthcare services for medical professionals.
