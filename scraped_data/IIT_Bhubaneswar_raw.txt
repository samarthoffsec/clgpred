### 3. Curriculum/Course Structure (Core Subjects)
The institute follows a "School" based system rather than traditional departments. Below are core subjects for major B.Tech branches:

- **Computer Science & Engineering (CSE):**
  - Operating Systems (CS3L005)
  - Database Systems (CS3L003)
  - Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  - Discrete Structures
  - Computer Organization and Architecture
- **Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE):**
  - VLSI Design (EC3L009)
  - Digital Signal Processing (EC3L003)
  - Communication Engineering (EC3L001)
  - Signals and Systems (EC2L002)
- **Electrical Engineering (EE):**
  - Electric Machines (EE3L001)
  - Control Systems (EE3L003)
  - Power Electronics (EE3L004)
  - Network Theory (EE2L001)
  - Power Systems (EE4L001)
- **Mechanical Engineering (ME):**
  - Thermodynamics
  - Fluid Mechanics
  - Theory of Machines
  - Heat and Mass Transfer
  - Manufacturing Technology
- **Civil Engineering:**
  - Solid Mechanics (CE2L001)
  - Structural Analysis (CE2L004)
  - Transportation Engineering (CE2L006)
  - Soil Mechanics (CE3L002)
  - Water Resources Engineering (CE3L003)

### 5. Startup Incubation
- **Official Incubator:** Research and Entrepreneurship Park (REP) / Technology Business Incubator (TBI).
- **Flagship Initiative:** **100-Cube Start-up Initiative** (Launched in 2024 to create 100 startups with ₹100 Cr valuation by 2036).
- **Facilities:** 80,000 sq. ft. of incubation space, seed funding up to ₹2.5 Lakh, dedicated labs, and IP/Patent support.
- **Notable Supported Startups:**
  - **Vasitars:** Specializes in pipeline repair and infrastructure monitoring.
  - **Twinverse Technology:** 3D visualization and digital twin solutions.
  - **Netravaan AI:** AI-driven computer vision analytics.
  - **Manati Mental Healthcare:** Digital mental health therapy.
  - **Carbonation India:** Carbon capture technology.

### 6. Wikipedia Cross-Verification
- **Campus Size:** Permanent residential campus spread over **936 acres** in Kansapada, Arugul (Khordha district).
- **Establishment:** Founded in 2008; shifted from mentor institute (IIT Kharagpur) to Bhubaneswar in 2009.
- **Status:** Institute of National Importance (since 2012).
- **International Ranking:** QS World University Rankings 2022 placed it in the 701–750 bracket globally.
- **Unique Feature:** Operates through "Schools" (e.g., School of Basic Sciences, School of Infrastructure) rather than standard departments.
