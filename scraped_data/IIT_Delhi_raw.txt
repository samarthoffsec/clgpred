### 3. Curriculum/Course Structure
* **Institute Core (All Branches):** Calculus, Linear Algebra & Differential Equations, Physics, Chemistry, Introductory Biology, Introduction to Computer Science, Introduction to Electrical Engineering, Engineering Mechanics, Product Realization through Manufacturing.
* **Computer Science and Engineering (CSE):** Data Structures, Analysis and Design of Algorithms, Operating Systems, Computer Architecture, Computer Networks, Database Management Systems, Discrete Mathematical Structures.
* **Electrical Engineering (EE):** Digital Electronics, Physical Electronics, Integrated Electronics, Circuit Theory, Signals and Systems, Control Engineering-I, Engineering Electromagnetics.
* **Mechanical Engineering (ME):** Engineering Thermodynamics, Introduction to Fluid Mechanics, Heat and Mass Transfer, Solid Mechanics, Kinematics & Dynamics of Machines, Manufacturing Processes.
