### **3. Curriculum / Course Structure (B.Tech)**
IIT Jodhpur follows a **Fractal-based Curriculum** allowing for modular learning and early interdisciplinary exposure.
*   **Common First-Year Subjects:** Mathematics I & II (Calculus, Linear Algebra), Physics, Chemistry, Introduction to Computer Science, Introduction to Electrical Engineering, Introduction to Bioengineering, Engineering Visualization, Engineering Design I & II, Communication Skills, and Social Connect.
*   **Core Subjects (CSE):** Data Structures and Algorithms, Discrete Mathematics, Computer Organization and Architecture, Operating Systems, Database Management Systems, Computer Networks, Theory of Computation.
*   **Core Subjects (EE):** Network Theory, Signals and Systems, Control Systems, Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Analog and Digital Electronics, VLSI Design, Microprocessors.
*   **Core Subjects (ME):** Thermodynamics, Mechanics of Solids, Fluid Mechanics, Kinematics and Dynamics of Machinery, Machine Design, Heat and Mass Transfer, Manufacturing Processes.
*   **Core Subjects (Bioengineering):** Cell and Molecular Biology, Human Physiology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Genetics, Biomaterials Engineering.

### **4. Campus Life & Infrastructure**
*   **Campus Area:** 852-acre self-sustainable residential campus in Karwar, Rajasthan.
*   **Sports Facilities:**
    *   **Indoor Stadium (Aakash Complex):** 8 Badminton courts, Squash courts, Table Tennis area.
    *   **Aquatics:** Swimming pool facility compliant with Inter-IIT standards.
    *   **Athletics:** Synthetic athletic tracks surrounding the primary football fields.
    *   **Outdoor Grounds:** Dedicated natural grass Cricket ground (15,000 sq. m.), Football fields, Hockey field, and multiple synthetic Tennis, Basketball, and Volleyball courts.
*   **Societies & Clubs:** 7 Societies under the **Students' Gymkhana** managing 42+ active clubs (e.g., Coding Club, Robotics, Aeromodelling, Groove Theory, Dramatics, Shutterbugs).
*   **Major Festivals:**
    *   **Ignus:** Annual Techno-Cultural Festival.
    *   **Varchas:** Inter-college Sports Festival.
    *   **Spandan:** Intra-college Cultural Festival.

### **5. Startup Incubation**
*   **Primary Incubator:** **Technology Innovation and Start-up Center (TISC)**, a Section 8 non-profit company.
*   **Specialized Hubs:** **iHub Drishti** (Technology Innovation Hub for Computer Vision, AR/VR) and **BIRAC BioNEST Centre** (Bio-incubation).
*   **Key Facilities:** 20,000+ sq. ft. of co-working spaces, advanced prototyping labs, plug-and-play wet labs, and seed funding support via MSME/MeitY grants.
*   **Notable Supported Startups:**
    *   **RKSH IMPACT:** Developed 'APM Signal' for ambulance green corridors.
    *   **Cellverse:** 3D bioprinting and digital twin platforms for medical research.
    *   **Niranthar Care:** Non-invasive continuous blood pressure monitoring devices.
    *   **NxGen Healtech:** Innovative 'iVac-Endo' dental irrigation device.
    *   **Divya Plasma Solutions:** Cold plasma-based indoor air sterilisers (tested at AIIMS).
