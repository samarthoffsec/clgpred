#### 3. Curriculum & Course Structure
IITH follows a unique **Fractal Academic Program** (modular credit system).

**Core Subjects by Branch:**
- **Common First Year:** Calculus, Linear Algebra, Modern Physics, Digital Fabrication, Introduction to Programming (C/Python), Environmental Chemistry.
- **Computer Science (CSE):** Discrete Structures, Data Structures, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Operating Systems (OS), Database Management Systems (DBMS), Computer Networks, Compilers, Theory of Computation.
- **Electrical Engineering (EE):** Signals and Systems, Control Systems, Digital Systems, Analog Electronics, VLSI Design, Semiconductor Device Fundamentals, Communication Systems, Electrical Machines.
- **Mechanical Engineering (ME):** Engineering Mechanics, Solid Mechanics, Fluid Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Manufacturing Science, Kinematics and Dynamics of Machines, Heat Transfer.
- **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Probability and Random Processes, Optimization Techniques.
- **Biomedical Engineering (BME):** Physio-anatomy, Bioengineering, Medical Imaging, Biomaterials.
