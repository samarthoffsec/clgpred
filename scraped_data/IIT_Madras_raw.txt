### 3. Curriculum/Course Structure
* **Common 1st Year Core Subjects:** Calculus, Physics (Classical/Quantum and Electromagnetism), Introduction to Programming, Engineering Mechanics, Basic Electrical Engineering, Engineering Drawing.
* **Computer Science and Engineering Core Subjects:** Discrete Mathematics, Programming and Data Structures, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Computer Organization and Architecture, Operating Systems.
* **Electrical Engineering Core Subjects:** Signals and Systems, Digital Systems, Electric Circuits and Networks, Microprocessor Theory and Lab, Engineering Electromagnetics.

### 4. Campus Life & Infrastructure
* **Sports Facilities:** Manohar C Watsa Stadium, IIT-Chemplast Cricket Ground, Olympic-standard Swimming Pool, Sitara Indoor Sports Complex, Gymkhana Complex, Floodlit outdoor fields for football and hockey.
* **Cultural Clubs (Sangam):** Music, Choreo, Thespian, Oratory, Writing, Quiz, Word Games, Fine Arts, Media, E-Sports, Comedy, Lifestyle, and Classical Arts.
* **Technical Clubs (Centre for Innovation - CFI):** 3D Printing, Aero, AI, Electronics, Horizon, iBot (Robotics), Product Design, Programming, and Webops & Blockchain.

### 5. Startup Incubation

* **Facilities:** IIT Madras Research Park, Centre for Innovation (CFI), Nirmaan Pre-incubator.
