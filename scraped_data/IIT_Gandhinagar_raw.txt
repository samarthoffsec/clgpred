#### **3. Curriculum & Course Structure (B.Tech)**
*   **Core Subjects by Branch:**
    *   **CSE:** Data Structures & Algorithms, Operating Systems, Computer Architecture, Discrete Mathematics, DBMS, Theory of Computation, Compilers, Networks.
    *   **EE (Covers ECE roles):** Signals & Systems, Electronic Devices, Control Systems, Power Systems, VLSI Design, Digital Signal Processing, Analog & Digital Circuits.
    *   **ME:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Solid Mechanics, Heat Transfer, Kinematics & Dynamics of Machines, Manufacturing Processes, Machine Design.
    *   **Civil:** Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Fluid Mechanics, Surveying.
    *   **Chemical:** Chemical Process Calculations, Thermodynamics, Reaction Engineering, Heat & Mass Transfer, Separation Processes, Process Dynamics.
    *   **Materials Engineering:** Structure of Materials, Thermodynamics, Physical Metallurgy, Mechanical Behavior, Materials Processing.
    *   **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** Machine Learning, Optimization Methods, Computer Vision, Probabilistic ML, Deep Learning.
    *   **Integrated Circuit Design & Technology (ICDT):** CMOS Circuit Design, IC Fabrication, Semiconductor Devices, Packaging.
*   **Unique Features:**
    *   **Foundation Programme:** A mandatory 5-week immersive induction for freshers focusing on creativity, ethics, and physical fitness.
    *   **Interdisciplinary Focus:** Flexible curriculum allowing minors in AI, Robotics, and Humanities.

#### **4. Campus Life & Infrastructure**
*   **Sports Facilities:**
    *   **Synthetic Track:** 400m IAAF-standard synthetic athletic track with facilities for high jump, pole vault, and javelin.
    *   **Aquatics:** Olympic-size swimming pool and separate indoor swimming pools.
    *   **Indoor Complex:** AC Gymnasium (24x7), Badminton courts, Squash courts, Basketball, and a Climbing Wall.
    *   **Outdoor Grounds:** Cricket ground (floodlit), Football ground, and Lawn Tennis courts.
*   **Student Clubs:**
    *   **Technical:** Mean Mechanics (Robotics), GRASP (Competitive Programming), SCALE (AI/ML), Odyssey (Astronomy), Metis (Software Development), CyberSentinel (Cybersecurity).
    *   **Cultural:** Roobaro (Music), Maffick (Dance), Abhinaya (Drama), Kodachrome (Photography).
*   **Sustainable Infrastructure:** India's first 5-star GRIHA LD (Green Rating for Integrated Habitat Assessment) campus; 5-star "Eat Right Campus" rating by FSSAI.

#### **5. Startup Incubation (IIEC)**
*   **Name:** IIT Gandhinagar Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center (IIEC).
*   **Presence:** Established as a Section 8 company (Technology Business Incubator).
*   **Key Facilities:** Co-working spaces, dedicated research labs, and prototyping grants (Nidhi Prayas program providing up to ₹10 lakhs).
*   **Notable Supported Startups:**
    *   **Cubeit:** Content-organization startup (Acquired by Myntra).
    *   **Infurnia:** Cloud-based architecture and interior design software.
    *   **Kairios Robotics:** Focused on automation and robotics solutions.
    *   **AeroSentinel Technologies:** Specializing in drone and aerial monitoring.
    *   **Agrocast Analytics:** Providing data-driven insights for agriculture.
